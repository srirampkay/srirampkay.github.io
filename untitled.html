Overview

Job Search can sometimes be very difficult to get through. From several interactions with job seekers and my personal encounter with job search, I've discovered that applicants do not have a prominant to track and analyze their job applications. They still use Whiteboards and Spreadsheets(shown below). This is a very manual process and can get extremely complex at times especially when applying hundreds of jobs over time. So, I decided to design a tool that aims to help candidates apply, track, document and analyze all their job applications in one place. There are companies like greenhouse and lever that are doing a great job with applicant tracking(for job posters).

Research

Users(Applicants) are using spreadsheets and whiteboards to keep track of active job applications and interviews. While this is pretty straightforward and manual, this could get really long and tiring when you apply hundreds of jobs.
