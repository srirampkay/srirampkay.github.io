Overview

Expiry has been operating under a “mystery box” subscription that lets users set their preferences (e.g. number of people, vegan, only produce) and allows them to place an order before noon for evening delivery.

Recently, Expiry has been losing customers. After doing user research, it turned out the customers want more control over their orders.

My task was to design an experience that allows customers to easily place same-day orders from a dynamic, limited supply of food.

Existing problems

So, Customers feel they don't have enough control over their orders. This could mean:

1. Customers may not always get the food they like

Existing design doesn't let users pick their food. It only asks for preferences like Vegan or Vegetarian. Though this maintains the idea of "Mystery" behind food delivery, This could result in upsetting customers during delivery.
Eg. Customer expects a "Vegan Salad" but gets "Vegan Sushi" instead.

2. Exclude allergies

Existing design doesn't ask for any specific food alergies before placing an order. This could result in delivery of food that the customer may be allergic to.
Eg. A customer who is lactose intolerant gets a cheese pizza.

3. No tracking options available

Existing design doesn't let users track their food. This could result in food left at the user's doorstep. If not notified, it could remain there for hours.

4. "Change of plans" for the customer

Due to unforseen reasons, the customer needs to change his plans for the evening. The current design doesn't let subscribers "pause" or "skip" delivery for that day. This could result in more food wastage and loss for the customer.
Eg:The customer gets invited to a birthday party for the evening and he/she needs to cancel delivery for that day

Scenarios

Primary solutions to Critical User Problems

Problem: 1. Customers may not always get the food they like
Solution: Show food options and allow users to select from that


Problem: 2. Exclude allergies
Solution: Include a text box to exclude allergies


Problem: 3. No tracking options available
Solution: Pick your preferred delivery slot


Problem: 4. "Change of plans" for the customer
Solution: Select "Not Today" to skip for the day
