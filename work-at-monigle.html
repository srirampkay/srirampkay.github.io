Takeaways

Priorities & Multitasking

First and foremost–it taught me how to better juggle multiple clients and priorities. Because again, on the corporate side, you’re going to be serving many projects. Just like you did on the agency side when you worked with 3-5 clients at a time. That’s a lot of juggling–and it’s taught me how to prioritize my work and my days in new and different ways.


Deadlines

A deadline’s a deadline. And you either hit it, or you can show yourself the door. That’s the attitude that’s often bred within agencies. And, they have to so they can compete in an increasingly competitive marketplace.


Knowing your bandwidth

When you think you’ve hit your limit, keep pushing. Then, push some more. This is something agency life taught me quickly. Instead of trying to manage your stress, push the bounds of it. You might be surprised how well you handle it.
